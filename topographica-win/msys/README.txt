MSYS and MinGW for building Topographica on Windows.

Unzip the archives to c:\msys and c:\MinGW, then create a shortcut to c:\msys\1.0\msys.bat, setting 'start in' to c:\msys\1.0\bin. That shortcut should give you
a shell suitable for building topographica (make win-msys-patch; make).
